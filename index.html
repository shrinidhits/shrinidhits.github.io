Shrinidhi Teganahally Sridhara

Hi, I am an incoming first-year PhD student in Computer Science at LaBRI, University of Bordeaux, France. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Adrian Tanasa and Prof. Felix Huber.

Prior to this, I completed my Masters in Computer Science at Chennai Mathematical Institute, Chennai, India, where I was advised by Prof. Geevarghese Philip.

My research interests lie in various areas of theoretical computer science, including algorithms, graph theory, and quantum computation.
